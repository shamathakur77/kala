Matrescence — The Becoming
मातृत्व-जन्म — नवी स्त्री
The word for what happens to a woman when she becomes a mother. You are neurologically rewired. Your identity fractures and reforms. Your old self grieves while the new one is still arriving. Nobody names this. Nobody maps it. Matrescence does — with Jungian psychology, Ayurvedic postpartum protocols, the 4th trimester, and the long slow return to self. For new mothers, for mothers of toddlers, for anyone still becoming. Coming soon.
